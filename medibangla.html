About MediBangla

MediBangla is a Bangladesh-focused medicine information platform designed to help patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals discover and compare medicines available in the Bangladeshi pharmaceutical market.

Our database includes medicines from leading Bangladeshi pharmaceutical companies including Square, Beximco, ACI, Incepta, Renata, ACME, Aristopharma, and many more.
